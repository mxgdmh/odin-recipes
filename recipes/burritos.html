Burrito Recipe

This could be your burrito

Ingredients

  • Fajitas
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Chop the onions
  3. Mix all ingredients (except fajitas in a bowl
  4. Warm up the tortillas
  5. Put the filling on the tortilla and carefully wrap it up
